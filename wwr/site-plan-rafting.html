Purpose

My purpose for this website is to create a place for anybody to ask any type of question relating to people already in or wanting to join the white water rafting community!

Audience

Our audience is for people who want to participate in white water rafting or have a question to ask. All are welcome.

We provide insured information that will help you on your river journeys!
